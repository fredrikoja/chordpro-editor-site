ChordPro Editor

A native macOS editor for ChordPro lead sheets

What is ChordPro?

ChordPro is a simple text format for writing songs with chords above the lyrics — used by musicians worldwide for lead sheets and songbooks.

Features

Live preview with chords above lyrics, syntax highlighting, transpose, song library, and iCloud sync with Pro.

Supported formats

Opens and saves .cho and .chopro files. Compatible with other ChordPro apps and tools.

Free vs Pro

Free: up to 5 songs in your library. Pro (one-time purchase): unlimited songs and iCloud sync.
